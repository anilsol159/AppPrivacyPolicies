Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to CoreFit! We're dedicated to helping you achieve your core strength and fitness goals while respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our abs-focused fitness application.

Your trust matters to us. We've designed CoreFit with privacy in mind, ensuring your fitness journey remains personal and secure.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Weight, height, body measurements, fitness goals, and other abs and core-related metrics you provide.
  • Authentication Data: Email address and profile information collected through secure authentication services.
  • Fitness Data: Core workout history, progress tracking, and performance metrics related to abdominal exercises.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with CoreFit, including exercise selections, workout completion rates, and app feature usage.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized core and abs workout plans tailored to your fitness level.
  • To track your progress and show improvements in your core strength and definition over time.
  • To authenticate users securely using our authentication system.
  • To improve app functionality and enhance the overall user experience.
  • To deliver customized nutrition recommendations that support your core fitness goals.

Data Storage

All your personal and workout data is securely stored on our cloud database infrastructure. We implement industry-standard encryption and security protocols to safeguard your information from unauthorized access.

Your core workout data is synchronized across your devices to provide a seamless experience while maintaining strict privacy controls.

Third-Party Services

CoreFit utilizes secure third-party services for authentication and data storage. These partners adhere to strict security standards and data protection regulations.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to any third parties. Any sharing of anonymized data is solely for the purpose of improving our services.

Your Rights

You maintain complete control over your personal data and have the right to:

  • Access your stored information at any time
  • Update or correct any of your personal details
  • Delete your account and associated data from our systems
  • Export your workout history and progress data

To exercise any of these rights, simply use the appropriate options in the app settings or contact our support team.

Data Security

We employ advanced security measures to protect your information, including:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Strict access controls for our development team
  • Automated threat detection systems

Policy Updates

We may occasionally update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make significant changes, we'll notify you through the app and update the "Last Modified" date below.
