Introduction

Thank you for using Victory Vault. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our application.

ASD Media ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. We have created this Privacy Policy to explain our data collection, use, and disclosure practices for the Victory Vault application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device or Other IDs: We collect device identifiers, including advertising IDs that are used by our advertising partners.
  • App Activity: We collect information about your interactions with the App, such as the features you use and the time spent using the App.

Local Storage

The App stores information locally on your device, including:

  • Activity names that you create
  • Timing information and records for your activities
  • Preferences for how activities are sorted and displayed

This locally stored information is used solely to provide the core functionality of the App and is not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App

The App includes advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect and process information about you to provide personalized advertising.

Google AdMob's use of information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, which can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

To learn more about how Google uses data from apps that integrate with its services, visit https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Google AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (including advertising identifiers)

Your Choices

Advertising Preferences

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • On Android: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Note that opting out of personalized advertising doesn't mean you won't see ads, just that they may be less relevant to you.

Deleting Your Information

You can delete all locally stored data in the App by:

  • Clearing the App's data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the App from your device

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information from our servers immediately.
