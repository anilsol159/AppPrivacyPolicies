Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Pocket Compass. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle any information collected when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Pocket Compass is designed with privacy in mind. Our app collects:

  • Location Data: We request location permissions to determine your geographic position for magnetic declination (True North) calculation. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.
  • Sensor Data: The app accesses your device's sensors (magnetometer, accelerometer) to provide compass functionality. This data is processed locally and is not stored or transmitted.
  • App Settings: Your preferences (True North toggle, Night Mode, Bearing Lock) are stored locally on your device for app functionality.

How We Use Information

The limited data we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Providing accurate compass orientation and direction
  • Calculating magnetic declination for True North functionality
  • Saving your app preferences
  • Improving app performance and user experience

Data Sharing and Transfer

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer your information to third parties. All data processing happens directly on your device.

Data Retention

Your settings are retained on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data. Location and sensor data are used in real-time and are not permanently stored.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Deny or revoke location permissions through your device settings
  • Clear all app data from your device's settings menu
  • Uninstall the app at any time

Children's Privacy

Pocket Compass is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
