HomeFit Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to HomeFit! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Weight, height, age, and other fitness-related data you provide.
  • Authentication Data: Your email address and profile information collected through Google Authentication.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as selected workout plans and BMI calculator usage.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalize workout plans and track your progress.
  • To calculate your BMI and offer tailored fitness recommendations.
  • To authenticate users securely using Firebase Authentication.
  • To improve app functionality and enhance user experience.

Data Storage

All data, including your personal and workout details, is securely stored on Firebase. We implement industry-standard measures to ensure data protection.

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase for authentication and data storage. Firebase complies with strict security protocols to safeguard your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your data at any time. If you wish to do so, please contact us through the app's support feature.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, feel free to reach out at contact@homefitapp.com.
