Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how SpendSense ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

SpendSense is designed to respect your privacy. We collect minimal information to provide you with the best possible experience:

  • Personal Data: The App does not collect any personally identifiable information. All budget data and expenses are stored locally on your device.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the App, such as feature usage and crash reports.
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device may be collected, such as device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App
  • To notify you about changes to our App
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • To improve the user experience

Data Storage

SpendSense stores all of your financial data locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your budget information, expenses, or categories on external servers. This means:

  • Your financial data remains private and stays on your device
  • Your data is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • If you uninstall the App, your data will be removed from your device

Permissions

SpendSense requires the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To alert you when you're approaching or exceeding your daily spending limit

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services, such as analytics providers, to help us improve the App. These services may collect information sent by your device. Any third-party service providers we use will be listed here:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics (anonymous usage statistics only)

Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
