Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18-7-2024

Thank you for choosing to use QuizzBuzz. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Android quiz app.

Information Collection and Use

QuizzBuzz does not collect any personal data from its users. The app fetches quiz question data from the OpenTrivia DB API, which provides public trivia questions.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the OpenTrivia DB API to fetch quiz questions. We do not control the content or policies of OpenTrivia DB, and we encourage you to review their privacy policy.

Data Security

Since QuizzBuzz does not collect any personal information, there is no personal data to protect. However, we still take the security of our app and the data it handles seriously.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
