Privacy Policy for FocusPom

Last updated: March 25, 2025

Introduction

FocusPom is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by FocusPom.

Information Collection and Use

FocusPom does not collect any personal information. All data, including your timer settings, task names, and session history, is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Permissions

FocusPom requests the following permissions:

  • Do Not Disturb Permission: Used only during active work sessions to help you focus without interruptions. This permission is optional.
  • Vibration Permission: Used to provide haptic feedback when interacting with the app, making for a more responsive user experience.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Timer settings (work duration, break duration)
  • Task names
  • Session history
  • Daily goals

No Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any information with third parties.

No Analytics or Advertising

FocusPom does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

FocusPom does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
